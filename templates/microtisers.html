Understanding Cost Per Mille (CPM)

CPM stands for "Cost Per Mille" (mille is Latin for thousand). It's the standard advertising metric that represents how much advertisers pay for 1,000 verified impressions.

Example: If an advertiser pays $10 CPM, they're paying $10 for every 1,000 people who see their ad. You earn $8 (80% of $10) for delivering those 1,000 verified impressions.
